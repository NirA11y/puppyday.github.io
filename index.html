Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority: PuppyDay stores all data locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

Data Storage

PuppyDay is designed with privacy at its core. All your puppy's information, including activity logs, growth data, photos, and personal notes, are stored exclusively on your iOS device using local storage mechanisms.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics from our users. The app functions entirely offline after download, ensuring complete privacy and control over your data.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to share with third parties. Your puppy's information remains private and secure on your device.

Device Security

Your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your iOS device updated and using appropriate security features like passcodes or biometric authentication.

Data Backup

If you use iCloud backup for your device, your PuppyDay data may be included in those backups according to your iCloud settings. This backup is managed entirely by Apple and follows their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. All data remains on the user's device.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be communicated through app updates. Since we don't collect user contact information, policy changes will be noted in the app and on this website.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@puppyday.app
